We are contributor in medical sciences by studying living systems and organisms for development and research purpose. We shape our student for thier bright future in thin field by proving knowledge and best practical facilities.

Labortory

The pillar strenght of the institute is its "Advanced Laboratory" set up the institute has acquired the latest equipments,tools & facilities for various Cells & Molecular Biology Lab,Industrial Biotechnology,Biochemistry,Microbiology,Plant Tissue Culture , Animal Tissue Culture And incubation chamber etc.

Library

The Library of MGM Institute of Biosciences & Technology was established in 2008. The institution has a state of art Library including the books from the fields of Life Sciences, Agriculture, Plant Biotechnology, Plant Breeding, Molecular Genetics, Biotechnology, Bioinformatics, Computer Sciences, Computational Biology, Mathematics, Statistics, Competitive Examinations books like UGC/CSIR etc.

Computer Lab

The computer lab equipped so that students may carry out productive research work & have constant access to information to facilate learning and growth.

Sports Fascility

The sports facility is an integral part of educational activity to keep students physically and mentally healthy for this, MGM has established huge sports complex with indoor and outdoor game facilities. The total area of sports complex is 97400 sq. m. out of which sports building area is 2151.61 sq. m. and area of courts is 18793.66 sq. M.
