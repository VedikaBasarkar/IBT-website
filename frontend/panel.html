The aim of education is to teach us how to think, than what to think, to improve our mind, think for ourselves, and not just fill our brains. Education makes a man a right thinker and a correct decision maker. In the age of Science and Technology, our aim is to build an institution of biotechnology and bioinformatics, an institution with marked difference in quality to develop students. We have excellent infrastructure, well equipped state of art laboratories. We are recognized as a fully functioning institute because we trigger our efforts to teaching and research to ignite the minds of students. So, join us and we will create a better future.

Vice Chancellor's Message

My dear young friends, Mahatma Gandhi Mission ( MGM) Charitable Trust is the mother body , which gave birth to the MGM University , Aurangabad. This is the historic decision of the Government of Maharashtra, which has passed the MGM University Act XXVI of 2019 to establish this self- financed university of Marathwada region of Maharashtra state. We the MGM family is so happy to inform you that the the foundation of the MGM University, Aurangabad took place during the 150 th Birth Anniversary Year of our philosophical and spiritual inspiration and father of the Indian nation Mahatma Gandhi.we shall move on this path of self-reliance and value-based higher education with mission of protecting our Mother Earth. Our humanity is facing and coping with deadly challenges of zoonotic diseases such as Covid-19 or Corona virus and in decades to come as per United Nations Environment Program ( UNEP ) scientific predictions many more such zoonotic diseases are going to attack us as we have encroached in to nature resultant in ecological imbalance and harm to biodiversity. I am sure human race shall learn a lesson from this health calamity of socio- economically disastrous pandemic.

In these challenging and threatening times, the importance of Biosciences and Biotechnology is noticed by the global academic community and leadership. To find out solutions to such pandemics the role of Biosciences and Biotechnology are going to be very valuable in protecting our humanity in terms of research & development in Biosciences, Biomedicine, Bioengineering, human genetics, Molecular & Cell Biology, Microbiology abd more importantly Biotechnology. Biotechnology is considered as the third wave in biological science and manifests an interface and interaction of basic and applied sciences ,where subtle as well as sometimes magical transformation of science in to technology can be experienced. This is very existing period in the areas Biosciences & Biotechnology. Our MGM is a prestigious educational brand in Maharashtra providing quality higher education with the world class infrastructure both academic and physical. The MGM University’s Institute of Biosciences & Biotechnology, popularly know as the MGM -IBT is a constituent research-focused institution with admirable placement rate . MGM-IBT’s alumni are performing well in the industry as well as in academia. Nationally reputed researcher Prof. Dr. Sanjay Harke is the Director of MGM -IBT and under his proactive leadership this institute is creating imprints in the area of Biosciences & Biotechnology. Apart from this , our MGM University’s eye catching green & clean campus designed & developed by our visionary Hon’ble Chancellor Ankushrao Kadam is ready to energise you and provides you knowledge culture, ragging free as well as healthy inspiring ambiance. I invite you to join this MGM -IBT family.

Established an Institute to pursue academic and research excellence. Establish an Institute state of the art physical and Intellectual excellence. To make Institute an attractive destination for researcher, scientist and students. Inspire the student community to become disciplined, passionate, ethical, practical oriented and scientific.
