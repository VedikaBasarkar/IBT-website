About Hostel

Hostel Rooms

Hostel rooms are bright, spacious, comfortable, clean and airy.Hostel room is provided with Single bed, Study table, Chair, Rack for stacking books, Cupboard. Hostel provides facilities for indoor games like Chess, Carrom ,etc. A television with cable Facility is also provided. Daily newspapers are an added facility for the residents.

Study Halls

Two study halls with adequate tables and chairs are available for hostel students with an environment to study.

Sick Room

A sick room with comfortable bed, blanket, furniture and a medical first aid box is available. Minimum two beds are provided.

Visitor's Room

Visiting room for the guests is available in the hostel with comfortable seating, lighting and toilet facilities.

Mess

Spacious dining area with adequate tables, chairs and drinking water with a well maintained and hygienic kitchen are available for students.

Laundry

A room is provided to a laundry person for ironing clothes of students during daytime. Clothes are given by students for washing at concessional rates.

Safety and Security

Safety and Security are prime areas of hostel activities. The hostel ensures appropriate controls for safety and protection of hostel inmates. Adequate provision of fire fighting equipments is placed on each floor of the hostel complex. First Aid box is provided for handling minor cuts/abrasion/burns. The box is available 24hours/day. Any student reported sick is immediately given attention by shifting the student to the MGM hospital. Provision of emergency alarm is made in the hostel. The switches for the same are made available on each floor.

Types of hostel

Girls Hostel A

Three seater rooms. Intake capacity is 494 students

Girls Hostel B

Single seater,Two seater,Four seater. Intake capacity is 140 students.

Girls Hostel C

Double seater AC rooms. Intake capacity is 208 students

Boys Hostel

Three seater rooms. Intake capacity 127 students

HOSTEL ADMISSION PROCEDURE

ADMISSION PROCESS

  • Admissions are given on first cum first basis as per availability of room. Students should collect the hostel admission form after confirmation of his/her college admission. Student should submit the hostel admission form complete in all respects.
  • Hostel fee is required to be deposited with hostel admission form.
  • The requisite hostel fee is accepted in the form of Demand Draft drawn in favor of "MGM's Hostel Aurangabad" from any bank.
  • After completing above formalities with Pass port size photos of student, parents and Local guardian. The admission form should be submit with the hostel in-charges & receive receipts of fees. Student's admission confirmed and room is allotted to students by handing the key of the room to the students.
  • At the time of admission, the student should be accompanied by his /her parents and local guardian if any who will take the responsibility of the students.
  • Demand Draft of the requisite fees is to be drawn in favor of "Mahatma Gandhi Mission's Hostel", Aurangabad.

ADMISSION CANCELLATION PROCESS

The Criteria of refunding the hostel fees for cancellation of hostel admission is as given:
  • If reported even for one day in the hostel after securing admission and applied within three months of admission, 25% of hostel fees is deducted and remaining amount is refunded.
  • If applied after three months of securing admission 100% of hostel fees is deducted and no fees is refunded.

HOSTEL RULES AND REGULATIONS

  • The academic year for the hostel will be considered from July to June. Student wishing to extend their stay after the said period (for any reason) will have to apply and pay separately.
  • Local Guardian cannot be changed or substituted throughout the whole year. For changing or substituting the local guardian, parents should personally introduce the new L.G. to the hostel authorities.
  • The student should deposit the hostel admission form at the beginning of the intended period along with the Demand Draft of requisite fees.
  • The hostel has been provided with the mess facility. The students have to take their food in the mess hall only.
  • Bringing outside Tiffin's is not allowed.
  • The students cannot claim for specific choice of their rooms.
  • Mutual exchange of room / room partner by the students is not allowed.
  • The Management shall have every right to increase / Decrease the hostel fees during the year.
  • Students are not allowed to keep more money or ornaments with them. If kept it is at their own risk. Complaints will not be entertained by the Management.
  • Ragging is strictly prohibited. The students who indulge in such activities and such other activities are liable for disciplinary action as are mentioned in the Maharashtra prohibition of Ragging Act, 1999 and will be expelled from the hostel immediately.
  • Disturbing other hostel mates, will invite severe punishment. It is expected that the students shall not engage themselves in any unlawful activities. Attendance is compulsory for all the students, which will be taken daily at 8.30pm. All the students should be present in the hostel premises by 8.30 pm. No student will be allowed to stay out after 8.30pm for any reason (tuition, dinner, Local Guardian.
  • While leaving the hostel campus and returning to the hostel, it is compulsory to enter the necessary information in the register kept at the hostel gate.
  • Guests / Parents / Relatives /Friends are allowed only up to the visitors room in the visiting hours only (4 pm to 7.30 pm). They will not be entertained during the day.
  • The hostel warden/supervisor/Management representatives have every right of Inspection.
  • The Management/ its representatives /the warden have every right to inform the parents / the guardians about the behavior and the conduct of the students during their stay in the hostel.
  • Students are not allowed to go on picnics / sight seeing without prior permission of the hostel authority /College authority
  • In case the hosteller requires staying with the Local Guardian, the Local Guardian with whom the hosteller intends to stay shall have to come personally to the hostel during office hours and apply for taking him / her with the prior permission of the authority. Only then the hostellerwill be permitted to leave the hostel campus. Rooms should be vacated before leaving the hostel.
  • Disobedience to the hostel warden /supervisors who reside in the hostel will invite severe punishment.
  • 18.The students should not use any electrical appliances (irons, heaters) in the rooms. If found, the appliances shall be forfeited and the student shall be liable for disciplinary action.
  • The students should read the notices displayed on the notice board regularly.
  • The students are liable to any damage of the furniture and fixtures provided to them in the room during their stay.
  • Furnished guest house is available for guest (mother/sister/friend) of any student who wishes to stay.
  • It is the responsibility of the students to strictly follow the above rules / instructions. If these are not followed or violated by the student the management shall have right to take disciplinary action, including the expulsion from the hostel
